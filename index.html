Seven Years Strong! Hack3r Runw@y Returns to Defcon 33!

Remember the awesome vibes of Defcon 31? We sure do! And now, after christening the LVC for Defcon 32, Hack3r Runw@y is back for its seventh incredible year at Defcon 33!

Awards will be handed out in 4 categories selected by esteemed judges and voters will select a final People's Choice winner:

  • Digital wearable - LED, electronic, passive
  • Smart wear - interactive, temperature sensing, mood changing, card skimmers, etc
  • Aesthetics and More (non-electronic) - 3d printed, geeky wear, passive design, obfuscation, cosplay
  • Functional wear - did you bling out your mask and/or shield, have a hazmat suit, lock pick earrings, cufflinks shims

Winners will be selected based on, but not limited to:

  • Uniqueness
  • Trendy
  • Practical
  • Couture
  • Creativity
  • Relevance
  • Originality
  • Presentation
  • Mastery

Categories

Digital Wearable

Incorporate passive technology into your wearable designs. (i.e. LEDs, audio, etc.)

Smart Wear

Functional and interactive elements in your creations. (i.e. sensors, actuators, etc.)

Aesthetics

Focus on the visual appeal and design elements. (i.e. 3d printing, cyberthemed, ect.)

Functional Wear

Emphasis on practicality and real-world applications. (i.e. lock picking tools, emergency kit, etc.)

Sign Up

Sign‑Up Window

Register your entry during the window below. Times shown in PDT.

  • Starts: June 28, 2026 — 5PM PST
  • Ends: August 8, 2026 — 12PM PST
  • Eligibility: Handmade or custom wearable items only — no store‑bought(ONLY) entries.

How to Sign Up

  1. Gather your photos and a short description of your wearable.
  2. Click "Enter_The_Contest" to open the registration form and submit details.
  3. Bring your physical entry to the showcase during DEF CON with badge. You can model or find someone to walk the runway for you.

Questions? Look at the form for email or DM me on Bluesky: @Hack3rRunway.bksy.social
